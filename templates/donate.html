Our Commitment to Our Donors

We will not sell, share or trade our donors' private information with other organizations. This policy applies to all information received by Chicago Autism Network, both online and offline, on any platform (including the Chicago Autism Network website and mobile applications), as well as any electronic, written, or oral communications.

When donations are processed through a third-party service provider, our donors’ personal information will only be used for purposes necessary to process the donation.

For more information regarding our Donor Privacy Policy, email [info@chicagoautismnetwork.org](info@chicagoautismnetwork.org).
