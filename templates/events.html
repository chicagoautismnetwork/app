Hang out at Cinespace

Cinespace Chicago Film Studios is a family-owned company based in Chicago specializing in the development, management and operation of studio space and support facilities for the film, television, and digital media production industry. For more visit chicagofilmstudios.com


Hear the Oak-Leyden Choir

Oak-Leyden Developmental Services helps children and adults with intellectual and developmental disabilities living in Western Cook County meet life’s challenges and reach their highest potential. They offer transformative programming in three areas: Children’s Services, Residential Services, and Lifelong Learning. The Oak-Leyden adult choir, Voices of Oak-Leyden, is made up of enthusiastic participants from Oak-Leyden’s Lifelong Learning Center in Oak Park. To learn more, visit www.oak-leyden.org
